A little about me!

My name is Yuri Chen and I'm a Computer Science major and Art and Design minor at the University of Michigan. I'll be graduating in April 2023. I'm currently working on a script that will help me balance teams as the League of Legends community director at UMich. Besides classes, I am also part of a volunteer program called Center for Sucess in Detroit that helps increase literacy in children. I love to help people and hope that my work will be about making things easier and more accessible for the rest of the population.

In my free time, I love to make art. It keeps me thinking creatively when in a static classes about computer science. I also like running outside, doing jigsaw puzzels, knitting, learning about video game design, and making music.
